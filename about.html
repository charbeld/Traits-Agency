About Us

We cast with intention.

Traits Agency is a boutique collective of casting directors, photographers, and producers who champion authentic stories. We collaborate with clients globally while keeping a minimal, people-first process.

What guides us

We value thoughtful collaboration over volume. Every project begins with deep discovery, making sure our roster reflects lived experiences, not trends.

Studios & locations

Our team works between Los Angeles, New York, and remote pop-up studios. Wherever the story needs to be told, we bring the right crew and energy.

How we partner

From short-form social edits to cinematic campaigns, we stay involved from shortlisting through final callbacks to ensure continuity and care.
